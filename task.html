POPULATION

According to 2011 census, Bodinayakanur had a population of 75,676 with a sex-ratio of 1,018 females for every 1,000 males,
The average literacy of the town was 76.18%, compared to the national average of 18.24%. The town had a total of 20333 households.

Government and politics

Bodinayakkanur (state assembly constituency) is part of Theni (Lok Sabha constituency).
In 2021 Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly election former Tamil Nadu Chief MinisterO. Panneerselvam was elected
from Bodinayakanur Constituency. As of 2021 he is the Deputy Leader of the Opposition.
